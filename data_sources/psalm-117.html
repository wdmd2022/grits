Psalm 117

Words: The Scottish Psalter

    1  O give ye praise unto the Lord,
          all nations that be;
       Likewise, ye people all, accord
          his name to magnify.
 
    2  For great to us-ward ever are
          his loving-kindnesses:
       His truth endures for evermore.
          The Lord O do ye bless.
